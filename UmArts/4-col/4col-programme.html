Title

In a forest there is an ongoing negotiation between nature and culture. This is visible not least in a forest subjected to industrial forestry. But even in a forest that we look upon as old-growth there are human traces.

Through artistic research I will examine different ways of looking at forests and forest landscapes and how these reach into our own time. Using Art as a way of expression I will investigate spaces in the forests tha have been shaped by a human presence.
